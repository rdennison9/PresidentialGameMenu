Development Progress

We are in the early stages of the development of our new game Election Simulator 2016. We want to create a game that accurately simulates the presidential election process. We will have candidates who participate in a primary process and go through all the steps an actual candidate would take. If your candidate makes it past the primary process then they will advance to the general election process with even more possibilities and oppurtunities.
